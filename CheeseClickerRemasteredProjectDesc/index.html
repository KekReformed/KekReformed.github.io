Cheese Clicker Remastered
Cheese Clicker (pictured in the top right) was a small game that I created whilst still in college, in which the player would click on a picture of cheese in order to gain more cheese. The player could use this cheese as a currency to buy upgrades that would automatically make cheese for them. Cheese Clicker Remastered (pictured in the bottom right) is a complete remake of that original project, created as a way to learn React.js, it includes lots of new upgrades, a save feature and a complete graphical redesign from the original
